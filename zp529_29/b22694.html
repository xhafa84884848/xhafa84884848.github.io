Our popular Rolltop bag is updated this season with a cleaner more efficient design. The padded laptop compartment is accessible from the outside via it’s own zipper compartment, the front of the bag has a larger more functional accessory pocket as well as loops to connect a Goal Zero solar panel to the outside. This season also features new colors and patterns and improved construction throughout.
